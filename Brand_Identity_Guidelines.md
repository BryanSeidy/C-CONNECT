# C-CONNECT — Brand Identity Guidelines (MVP v1.0)

> **Brand Positioning**
>
> **C-CONNECT** is the digital infrastructure for Cameroon's local economy.
> It connects producers, artisans, SMEs, cooperatives, women-led businesses, wholesalers and professional buyers through a premium digital marketplace built around trust, authenticity and local economic growth.
>
> The brand should feel modern enough to compete with international marketplaces while remaining deeply rooted in Cameroonian identity.

## AI Design Instructions

When generating interfaces for C-CONNECT, always follow these principles:

- Premium minimalism over decoration.
- Use generous whitespace and a calm visual rhythm.
- Prioritize real photography of Cameroonian producers, artisans, SMEs, agricultural workers, and women entrepreneurs.
- Combine modern SaaS aesthetics with subtle African-inspired geometric accents.
- Favor rounded cards, soft shadows, clean grids, and refined typography.
- Use the brand palette consistently, with green as the dominant color and gold only as an accent.
- Avoid gradients unless they are extremely subtle.
- Keep layouts conversion-focused and content-first.
- Design every page to inspire trust, professionalism, and national pride.
- Ensure every component is responsive, accessible, and scalable.
- The overall visual quality should be comparable to Stripe, Airbnb, Apple, Linear, and Notion while expressing a distinctive Cameroonian identity.
